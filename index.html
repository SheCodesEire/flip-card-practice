Welcome.

The Vegan Van is a small food truck based in Dunfanaghy, North Donegal.

We believe that everyone should have access to affordable, high-quality meals that don't cost the earth.

Whether you need to re-fuel after a long hike, strenuous surf or simply want to enjoy a nutritious and affordable lunch, we’ve got the food for you!

Menu

Better for our world, better for you.

Eco-Friendly

We believe in protecting our environment for future generations. We only use 100% recyclable packaging and provide an on-site recycling bay for all waste products. We offer a 5% discount to all customers who cycle, walk or carpool to our food truck.

Community

We source all our ingredients from local suppliers. Our “enabling excellence” initiative offers paid work experience to young people with complex social or medical backgrounds from Co. Donegal.
